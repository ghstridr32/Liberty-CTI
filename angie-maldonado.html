The Story

Built in the rooms where
the decisions actually happen.

Angie Maldonado began her Air Force career as a Russian Language Specialist — the same analytical foundation as her co-founder — and spent three decades earning assignments that placed her in the rooms where the most consequential national security decisions are made.

Her career ran from combat targeting in Qatar to special operations intelligence, from senior Pentagon staff to the most prestigious staff role available to a military officer: Military Assistant to the Deputy Secretary of Defense.

The E-Ring of the Pentagon is not a destination for the merely capable. It is where the military's most trusted officers serve the nation's most senior civilian leaders. Angie Maldonado earned that trust through decades of demonstrated performance — and she carried that standard into Liberty CTI.

Liberty CTI is built on both careers. That is not a marketing claim — it is the structural reality that separates this firm from every competitor.

// White House · Washington, D.C.

Perspective

What this experience
delivers to clients.

Experience matters only if it improves decisions. Angie’s background translates into clear value for organizations that need disciplined judgment, trusted execution, and leadership-level perspective.

Trusted performance at the highest levels — Operate with the discretion, reliability, and judgment required in senior Pentagon environments where mistakes carry strategic consequences.
Calm decision support under pressure — Bring structure and clarity to fast-moving situations so leaders can focus on decisions instead of noise.
Operational discipline that scales — Apply the same rigor used to lead large training and mission-support organizations to commercial risk and readiness challenges.
