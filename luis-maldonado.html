The Story

An unorthodox path.
An uncommon standard.

Luis Maldonado started in July 1997 as an enlisted Russian Linguist — learning to listen, analyze, and produce intelligence under conditions that left no margin for error. That origin shapes everything about Liberty CTI.

He commissioned in 2009 as a distinguished graduate of Officer Training School. What followed was three decades across ISR, cyber, electronic warfare, special operations, and national security policy — from combat deployments in Afghanistan and Qatar to the highest staff positions in the U.S. government.

Inside the White House Situation Room, he delivered over 300 intelligence products to the nation's most senior national security leadership. He then moved to the NSC as Director for Legislative Affairs — coordinating interagency engagement with Congress on the full range of the President's national security priorities.

Liberty CTI exists because that perspective belongs in the private sector. Texas's most critical industries face the same adversaries government agencies track daily — but receive none of the same warning.
