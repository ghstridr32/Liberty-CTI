Our Vision

Helping Texas Leaders
See Across the Next
Disruption.

The threat landscape facing Texas's most critical industries is shaped by more than opportunistic cybercrime. It is shaped by geopolitical competition, criminal ecosystems, supply-chain concentration, and cross-sector dependencies that can turn one incident into a much broader business disruption.

Organizations must understand these dynamics not as background context, but as decision advantage that informs investment, governance, communications, and operational risk posture. The question is no longer whether your sector is a target. The question is whether leadership sees the risk early enough to act.

Strategic warning is not a luxury. In the current environment, it is a requirement for protecting operations, preserving revenue, and avoiding executive surprise.
